About

Our Story

Domingo's Outside Recs started as an idea and soon became a reality in April 2024. Founded by Cal Poly Pomona (CPP) student, Larissa Domingo, the company aims to provide outdoor activity recommendations to inspire people to enjoy the outdoors, especially as summer approaches.

Our goal is to encourage individuals to disconnect from electronic devices and engage in outdoor activities that promote physical and mental well-being.

Our Mission

We aim to create a community and platform for individuals seeking outdoor activities and adventures. In a world dominated by electronic devices, we strive to remind people of the joy and benefits of outdoor experiences.

Our Founder

Larissa Domingo is a fourth-year college student at California Polytechnic State University - Pomona (CPP) double majoring in Computer Information Systems and Marketing. Her passion for data and marketing led her to pursue a career as a Data Analyst or Marketing Analyst within the sports industry, combining her love of sports and analytics.

At CPP, Larissa works at Associated Student Inc. (ASI) as a Social Media and Marketing Assistant, serves on the Data Analytics Team with Management Information Systems Student Association (MISSA), and helps the CPP Volleyball Sports Club at CPP as the Club Secretary.

Outside of school, Larissa enjoys bingeing drama series, watching Survivor, playing volleyball, and spending time with friends. She loves any excuse to celebrate life and going out, whether it be going on a roadtrip or going out to eat! Her drive to share her favorite outdoor spots and memories inspired her to create Domingo's Outside Recs.
