Hiking

Eaton Canyon

Location: 1750 N Altadena Dr Pasadena, CA 91107

Description: Going to Eaton Canyon after it rains is the VIBE! Expect to get your feet wet and a reward of a beautiful natural waterfall.

Hollywood Sign

Location: Hollywood Hills Los Angeles, CA

Description: There's something about the summershine, baby! It's a hollywood dream, featuring a 6 mile trail loop to see the iconic Hollywood sign. This is a MUST if you live in SoCal.

Easy Street

Location: 3605 Blue Gum Dr Yorba Linda, CA 92886

Description: A local trail that homes frogs, bunnies, rattle snakes, and spiders. This trail feels like an escape from the city even though it really isn't!

Top of the World

Location: Quarter Horse Dr Yorba Linda, CA 92886

Description: Get a 360 degree view of the city of Yorba Linda. To the right is the high school and to the left is the sun setting.

Panorama View

Location: 2000 E Bastanchury Rd Fullerton, CA 92835

Description: Here lies a bench and patch of grass with the perfect view for a perfect sun set.
