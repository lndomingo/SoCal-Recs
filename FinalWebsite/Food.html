Food

Locally-owned

Won Kok Dim Sum

Location: 210 Alpine St Los Angeles, CA 90012

Description: The BEST and CHEAPEST dim sim you can find. Won Kok Dim Sum sells food less than a dollar each item. This small business can be found in the heart of LA. It's just a window and always has a line no matter the time of day because it's THAT good!

Ruby BBQ Food

Location: 9567 E Garvey Ave Unit 1&2 South El Monte, CA 91733

Description: A cash only, family owned business. Order anything here and you can never go wrong. Everything is amazing and the owners are the sweetest.

Fratellinos

Location: 600 S Brea Blvd Brea, CA 92821

Description: A locally-owned italian restraunt. Originally named Lomelli's turned to Fratellino's. If you need to try anything, try the cappuccino ice cream! It's coffee ice cream with REAL coffee beans!!

Avalon Bagels

Location: 16961 E Imperial Hwy Yorba Linda, CA 92886

Description: My go-to whenever a friend asks, "Where should we go?" With only two locations, Avalon Bagels is a town favorite. From the fresh smoothies to the bacon melts, everything on the menu is a must have.
